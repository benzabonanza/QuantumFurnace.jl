Refactor TrajectoryFramework to remove the embedded mutable workspace, making the framework read-only during stepping. Introduce explicit `ws::TrajectoryWorkspace` and `rng::AbstractRNG` parameters throughout the call chain. Add `TrajectoryResult` struct for structured returns from `run_trajectories`.

Purpose: Gate requirement THRD-01 -- workspace separation is the prerequisite for all multi-threading work in v1.2. The framework must be safely shareable across threads while each thread owns its own workspace and RNG.

Output: Modified `src/trajectories.jl` with refactored structs and functions, updated `src/QuantumFurnace.jl` exports.
