  \caption{Unified phase-estimation / operator-Fourier-transform forward-half
  circuit with $r$ ancilla qubits and a bundled system register $\ket{\psi}$. The
  state-preparation block $\mathbf{Prep}_f$ loads the time-domain window
  $\ket{f} = \sum_{\bar t\in S_{t_0}} f(\bar t)\ket{\bar t}$ on the ancilla register;
  specialising to the uniform window $f\equiv 1/\sqrt{N}$ reduces it to a tensor
  product of Hadamard gates on each qubit (standard QPE), while a Gaussian
  (or Gevrey) window implements the forward half of the operator Fourier
  transform. Each ancilla then controls $U^{2^k}$ with $U = \ee^{+\ii H t_0}$
  and $k = 0,\ldots,r{-}1$, and the QFT (physics-forward convention, with
  kernel $\ee^{-\ii\bar t\bar\omega}/\sqrt{N}$) maps the accumulated phases to
  the frequency register $\ket{\bar\omega}$ for readout or for a subsequent
  diagonal action $\gamma(\bar\omega)$ on the ancilla before uncomputation.}
